JOIN US

Evermay is an export-oriented enterprise specializing in garment and textile sales, offering a broad array of clothing and accessories with significant markets in Australia, the US, and beyond. We are currently expanding and looking to welcome skilled individuals to our growing team.

Responsibilities:
  1. 1. Execute the company's trade operations, adhere to trade procedures, and cultivate new markets.
  2. 2. Responsible for engaging with clients, preparing quotations, participating in business negotiations, and contract signing.
  3. 3. Oversee production tracking, shipment coordination, and on-site supervision of loading.
  4. 4. Conduct document verification, customs clearance, financial settlements, and provide after-sales service.
  5. 5. Expansion and maintenance of client relationships.
  6. 6. Organization and archiving of business-related documentation.
  7. 7. Reporting on business-related activities.

Requirements:
  1. 1. Bachelor's degree or higher in International Trade, Business English, or related fields.
  2. 2. Minimum of two years experience in the trade sector, with a preference for candidates with experience in foreign enterprises.
  3. 3. Proficient in trade operational procedures and relevant legal regulations, with professional knowledge in the field of trade.
  4. 4. Ability to communicate fluently in English via email with clients.
